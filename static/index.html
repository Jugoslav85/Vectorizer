Pre-processing
4
Edge-preserving filter. Reduces noise inside colour regions without touching boundaries. 0 = off.
3
Removes speckle noise while keeping hard edges sharp. 1 = off, 3 = mild, 5 = strong.
3
Closes small gaps in lineart strokes. 1 = off. Only affects Line Art mode.
Colour simplification
32
Hard palette cap before tracing. Lower = fewer colour layers, faster, bolder. 0 = use posterize instead.
12
Merge near-identical colours before tracing. Higher = more aggressive merging, fewer paths. 0 = off.
6
Colour banding (used when Max colours = 0). Lower = flatter shapes. Higher = more tonal gradients.
Tracing
6
How precisely colours are matched. Higher = more accurate, more paths.
4
Colour difference needed to create a separate shape. Lower = more shapes.
6
Removes tiny stray paths. Higher = cleaner but may lose fine detail.
48
Higher = fewer corners, smoother curves. Lower = more angular, more detail.
70
How aggressively adjacent curve segments are merged. Higher = fewer, longer, smoother splines.
Post-processing
0.3
Removes redundant path points on straight segments. Higher = fewer points, smoother output.
